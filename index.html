What you see blurs thelines of reality and imaginary! The face a well-known celebrity busy in a makeupsession, choosing products and teaching you how to do it right on a digital screen. Her eyes blink and a link opens.You think, she really exists at that moment. The 5 minutes that you are stuck on the road prods you to swipe the phone, open that application, click the link received from the digital screen and Voila!!!You are exactly into the same booth interacting with the lady herself from the comfort of your car enhancing your knowledge about makeup.

    This is when the blend of digital & technology immerses you in an experience like never and in real time. What if you see yourself sitting in a car(virtually) you wish to buy, on a device? The I pad for e.g.; first takes you on a test drive showcasing the car’s features etc. and then asks you to physically sit in it. Isn’t that awesome? Often the first touch and feel experience itself decides your next steps and brings the product closer to your reach.
